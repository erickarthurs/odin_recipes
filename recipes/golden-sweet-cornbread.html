Golden Sweet Cornbread

Description

If yoou like sweet corn bread, this is the recipe for you! My mom made this for me as a child, now it's my family's favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yello cornmeal
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F(200 degrees C). Spray or ightly grease a 9 inch round cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt and baking powder. Stir in egg, milk and vegetable oil until well combined. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
